ShieldX Account Deletion Request

At ShieldX, we respect your privacy and your right to manage your data. If you wish to delete your ShieldX account and all associated personal data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Send an Email: Please send an email to our support team at sumoproductivityexperts@gmail.com.
  2. Subject Line: Use "ShieldX Account Deletion Request" as the subject of your email.
  3. Include Your Information: In the body of the email, please provide the exact email address or username associated with the ShieldX account you wish to delete. This helps us verify your request.
  4. Confirmation: Upon receiving your request, we will send a confirmation email to the address provided. Once confirmed, your account and data will be processed for deletion.

Data Deletion and Retention Policy:

Upon successful account deletion request, ShieldX will delete the following personal data associated with your account:

  • User Profile Information: Your name, email address, and any other personal details provided during account creation.
  • App Settings & Preferences: All custom settings, blocking rules, profiles, schedules, and configurations you have set up within the app.
  • Usage History: Records of app interactions, blocked events, and web browsing history collected by ShieldX.
  • Device Data: Any device-specific identifiers or information linked to your account.

Please note that certain types of data may be retained for a limited period, as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Anonymized Crash Reports: Non-identifiable crash logs and diagnostic data may be retained for up to 90 days for app improvement and debugging purposes.
  • Billing Records: Transaction and billing information related to any premium subscriptions will be retained as required by financial and legal regulations. This data is not linked to your personal usage but to the transaction itself.

Your account and associated deletable data will be permanently removed from our active systems within 7 business days of confirming your deletion request. Please be aware that it may take additional time for data to be completely purged from backup systems.
